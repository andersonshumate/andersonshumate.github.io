Cold Opens: A History of the Late-Night Monologue

This site will analyze the trajectory of the late-night monologue, from Steve Allen on The Tonight Show in 1954, to the Home Monologue during the COVID-19 pandemic. What makes a monologue unique? A memorable joke? The delivery by the host? The socio-political context? The fact that the host stumbled walking to their desk?
